Fun Media Playlist Maker

This is a fun, simple media playlist maker that you can use to organize and enjoy your favorite music. The process is incredibly simple, just follow the three easy steps below to get started right away!

1. Upload your files

Use our easy drag-and-drop file uploader to put your music files into a place where you can organize them by artist, album, and song name.

2. Make a playlist

Choose from your music library to make a playlist and sort it however you like, even randomize for an eclectic experience!

3. Listen online!

After you make your playlist, just hit play and we'll do the rest. You can just sit back and enjoy your favorite music.
